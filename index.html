Types Of Trucks

Mini Truck
(4-Wheeler)

Perfect for small businesses and local deliveries. Ideal for navigating narrow streets and urban areas. Capacity up to 1.5 tons. Best for furniture, home shifting, and small commercial goods.

Medium Truck
(6-Wheeler)

Versatile medium-sized transport solution. Suitable for intercity logistics and medium cargo. Capacity up to 4 tons. Ideal for retail distribution, construction materials, and medium commercial loads.

Heavy Truck
(10-Wheeler)

Heavy-duty transportation for large cargo. Perfect for long-distance and bulk materials. Capacity up to 16 tons. Best for industrial goods, bulk commodities, and large-scale logistics operations.
